Dragon Ball Collector!!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four dragon balls below. By clicking on a dragon ball you will add a specific amount of points to you total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each dragon ball is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game start, the game will change the values of each dragon ball.
